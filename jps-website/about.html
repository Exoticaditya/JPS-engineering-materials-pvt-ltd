About JPS Engineering

Delivering precision engineering solutions since our inception.

Our Story

Excellence in Engineering Plastics

JPS Engineering Material Pvt Ltd is a dynamic leader in the manufacturing and supply of high-performance engineering plastics and metal components. Located in the industrial hub of Greater Noida, we leverage cutting-edge technology and decades of expertise to serve a diverse client base.

Our core strength lies in our ability to customize solutions. Whether offered as semi-finished shapes or fully machined parts, our materials—ranging from Cast Nylon (PA6 G) to specialized composites—are designed to withstand the toughest production environments.

"We don't just supply materials; we provide engineering solutions that enhance efficiency and longevity."

Our Mission

To provide superior quality engineering materials that replace conventional metals, reducing weight and maintenance costs while improving performance.

Our Vision

To be the global benchmark in the engineering plastics industry, recognized for innovation, quality, and customer-centricity.
